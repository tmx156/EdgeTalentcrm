Alex AI Integration

WhatsApp AI Sales Agent — CRM Integration

New Feature

Overview

We've integrated Alex, an AI-powered WhatsApp sales agent, directly into the CRM. Alex automatically contacts leads via WhatsApp, qualifies them through a structured sales conversation, handles objections, and guides them to book an appointment — all without manual intervention.

The integration is two-way: the CRM sends leads to Alex, and Alex reports back with real-time status updates, conversation summaries, and booking confirmations. Everything is tracked in a dedicated Alex AI Dashboard inside the CRM.

How It Works

1

Send Lead to Alex

A CRM user opens a lead's detail page and clicks "Send to Alex AI". The lead's name, phone (formatted to +44), email, and a personalised booking link are sent to Alex.

↓
2

Alex Contacts the Lead via WhatsApp

Alex initiates a WhatsApp conversation and runs a structured qualification script: Hook → Connection → Qualification → Education → Close. All progress is reported back to the CRM in real-time.

↓
3

Lead Books via the Booking Page

When ready, Alex shares a personalised booking link. The lead opens it and sees a premium booking page connected to the live CRM diary — they pick a date, time, confirm their details, and book.

↓
4

Booking Confirmed in CRM

The booking appears instantly in the CRM diary. A confirmation is sent to the lead via SMS and email using the existing booking confirmation template. The lead's status updates to "Booked" across the CRM and the Alex dashboard.

Lead Status Tracking

As Alex works through the conversation, the lead's status updates in real-time on the dashboard:

New → Qualifying → Objection → Booking Offered → Booked

If Alex encounters a complex situation, the lead is escalated:

API Endpoints Built

Calendar API (for Alex to query during conversations)

GET /api/calendar/available-slots?date=YYYY-MM-DD&days=N
POST /api/calendar/book
DELETE /api/calendar/book/:bookingId

Webhook (two-way communication)

POST /api/replydesk-webhook/trigger/:leadId — Send lead to Alex
POST /api/replydesk-webhook/update — Receive status updates from Alex

Booking Page (public, no auth)

GET /api/public/booking-alex/availability
GET /api/public/booking-alex/lead/:identifier
POST /api/public/booking-alex/book/:identifier
